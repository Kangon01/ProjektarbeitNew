The Software enumerates a Camera Link camera, but fails to connect it.

Question

What should I do if the Software enumerates a Camera Link camera, but fails to connect it?

Possible Cause

The Camera Link camera has been connected to another program.

Solution

Disconnect the camera from the program, and then connect it to the Software again.
