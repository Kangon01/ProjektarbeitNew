Live View Control

You can start the live view of the connected camera to view the real-time image information, including the resolution, RGB values, hue, and saturation.

Before you start the live view, you need to connect to the camera. Click to show the Camera List. In the Camera List, double-click a camera to connect to it.

On the image window, click the buttons to control the live view.
