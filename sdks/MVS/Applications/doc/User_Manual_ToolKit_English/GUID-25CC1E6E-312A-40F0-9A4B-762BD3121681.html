Tool

You can view and export log information which records your operations on the Software. For each log, you can view its type, time, and event.

  1. Go to Tool > Log to open the Log Information pane.
  2. Select a log type.
  3. Select a time period during which the logs were generated.
  4. Click Search.
  5. Optional: Click Export to export log information to the PC.
